1. What Information Do We Collect?

Personal Information You Disclose To Us

In Short: We collect personal information that you voluntarily provide to us.

If you contact us for support or feedback, we may receive your email address and any information you choose to include in your message. We use this information only to respond to your request and maintain support records as needed.

Sensitive Information

Ambient Audio includes optional sleep and audio features that may involve sensitive information. This may include sleep sessions, Apple Health sleep stage data accessed with your permission, microphone sleep audio recordings, and custom sound recordings. This information is stored locally on your device unless you choose to share it with us, such as by including details in a support email.

Payment Data

If you purchase a subscription, payment is processed by Apple through the App Store. We do not receive or store your full payment card details. Apple may process payment information according to Apple's Privacy Policy and App Store terms. Apple's Privacy Policy is available at https://www.apple.com/legal/privacy/.

Application Data

If you use Ambient Audio, the app may request permission to access the microphone and Apple Health sleep data. You can grant or revoke these permissions in iOS Settings, the Health app, or within Apple permission prompts.

We do not collect your GPS location, contacts, camera photos, social media profile information, or government identifiers. We do not use third-party advertising, remarketing, or analytics tools.
